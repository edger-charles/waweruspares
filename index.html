About Us

A story of Success

At Waweru General Auto Spares, we take pride in delivering premium ex-Japan vehicle parts for over a decade. Our extensive range of services includes expert garage repairs, thorough car wash options, and seamless vehicle importation tailored to our customers' needs. Conveniently located on Biashara Street in Nakuru, we are committed to maintaining transparency in all our transactions, offering affordable prices without compromising on quality. Our focus on reliability ensures that our customers receive the best products and services, guaranteeing satisfaction every step of the way as we support your automotive needs.
